üìã Discount Rules - No Claims

  • 2 Years Claims-Free (Under Rate): 15% discount on gross rate
  • 2 Years Claims-Free (Under Min Premium): 20% discount on min premium
  • 1 Year Claims-Free (Under Rate): 10% discount on gross rate
  • 1 Year Claims-Free (Under Min Premium): 10% discount on min premium
  • Always compare with last year's rate/premium and use the lower value

‚ö†Ô∏è Loading Rules - Faulty Claims

  • Claim ‚â§ 50% of premium: No loading (current rate + depreciation)
  • Claim 51-100% of premium: 10% loading
  • Claim 101-200% of premium: 20% loading
  • Claim >200% or >1 claim: 30% loading or refer to Senior UW
  • Claim >300% or >2 claims: Refer to HOD (mandatory)
  • 2+ Own Damage claims: Offer TPL coverage only

üìâ Depreciation Schedule

  • 1st Year: 20% depreciation
  • 2nd Year: 15% depreciation
  • 3rd Year onwards: 10% depreciation per year
  • Age calculated from manufacture year, not registration date

üöå Special Bus Rules

  • Discounts applied to net rate (not total premium)
  • Loadings applied to basic rate (excluding additional benefits)
  • Dealer repair: Only 1st year (vs 3 years for private vehicles)
  • 2nd year onwards dealer repair: Refer to HOD

üîß Dealer Repair Rules

  • Private (Years 1-3, no claims): Auto-approved
  • Private (Year 4 or with claims): Refer to Senior UW
  • Private (Year 5+ or with claims): Refer to HOD
  • Bus (Year 1, no claims): Auto-approved
  • Bus (Year 2+ or with claims): Refer to HOD
